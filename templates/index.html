WOMEN IN TECH

The State of Women In Technology Roles

GitHub Repo

OVERVIEW

Despite the growth in Technology jobs, the number of women in the United States occupying roles in Technology is significantly less than their male counterparts. When we look at the women's representation in the Technology roles, specifically women in Software Engineering, the data science field, the gender imbalance remains true.

QUESTION

What is the difference between the percentage of the women (Kaggle survey respondents) population and men (Kaggle survey respondents) population who occupied Technology roles in Software Engineering, Machine Learning, and Data Science in the United States?
When comparing men and women in similar positions, what factors impact does a pay gap exist? Do years of coding experience and education influence the salary of women?



OBJECTIVE

In Our analysis, we compared the 2020 Kaggle survey results against the previous two years survey to determine the key differences between men & women within the dataset to reject the null hypothesis and conclude that a gender and pay gap imbalance exists.
In our analysis, we utilized Scikit-learn machine learning library for Python to model the relationship between salary and Age, Formal Education & Years of Coding using a supervised linear Regression method.
